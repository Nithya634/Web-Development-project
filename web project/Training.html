Training

Training and development is a vital part of human resource development. It is assuming an ever-important role as a result of the advancement of technology that has led to increased competition, increased expectations of customer quality and service and a subsequent need to reduce costs. It is also more important globally to prepare workers for new jobs. In the current wording, we will focus more on the emerging need for training and development, its implications for individuals and employers.

Our online training management system helps you streamline and standardize online training in more than one way. It is a learner-centric software application hosted in the cloud. The training courses created and designed using Training Maker offer anytime, anywhere access to authorized users. The automated tracking and reporting feature makes it easier for trainers to track each trainee’s progress.
